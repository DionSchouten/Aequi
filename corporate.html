About Aequi

Our Vision

In a society where physical health is the ultimate goal, and the night and party lifestyle is labelled unhealthy and toxic, we believe that only if one allows themselves the best of both worlds, will they live a balanced lifestyle to make them fully happy.


Our Values

- Happiness

- (Un)Healthy

- Self-growth

Our Mission

Our mission is to simply share knowledge regarding balance with people and spread awareness to create a positive mindset and attitude towards life, and thus achieve true happiness.
